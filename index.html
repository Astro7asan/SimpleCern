Metaphor Legend

Photon (The Skier) Glides seamlessly over the snow without sinking. It ignores the Higgs field entirely, maintaining zero mass and flying at the speed of light.
Electron (The Walker) Sinks slightly, feeling minor resistance. This drag is interpreted as light atomic mass.
Top Quark (Deep Boots) Gets heavily bogged down in the snow. Fighting the field's drag represents its massive weight.
THEORY NOTE If field density drops to zero, all particles speed off synchronously at lightspeed, showing why mass is a dynamic property, not an intrinsic constant.
